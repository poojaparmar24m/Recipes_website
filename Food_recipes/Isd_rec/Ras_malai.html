ras malai

Rasmalai is yet another one of my favorite sweets.Ras malai is a popular Indian sweets consisting of white cream, sugar, milk, and cardamom-flavored paneer cheese known as chhana.Ras means “juice” or “juicy”. Malai means “cream” and refers to thickened milk filled with clotted cream.

This creamy thickened and sweetened milk is also known as Rabdi. This delicious dessert is most popular during the festivals such as Holi and Diwali.Ras malai is of West Bengali origins, and is sometimes described as a rich cheesecake without a crust.

ingredients
  • 12 to 15 Rasgulla – homemade or store brought ones
  • 1 litre whole milk
  • 4 to 5 tablespoons sugar or add as required
  • 10 to 12 almonds unsalted – blanched
  • ▢10 to 12 pistachios unsalted – blanched
  • ½ cup water for blanching the almonds and pistachios
  • ½ teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons rose water or kewra water (pandanus water)
  • 8 to 10 saffron strands
  • few saffron strands – for garnish
